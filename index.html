A small forge of original worlds, all sharing one engine and one identity layer. Walk through forests where other players have left marks. Hunt, craft, learn skills, solve riddles.

Type commands. Walk between rooms. Trade with strangers, fight beside them, or leave them mysteries to find. Your character lives on a Nostr key — write it down and you can return on any device, anywhere.

▶ PLAY

What it is

  • A platform of persistent, asynchronous, multiplayer text adventures — played in your browser, no install.
  • Multiple worlds ready to explore: bundled stories ship in the engine, more are discoverable over Nostr.
  • Other players share your world. You may pass them in the woods, or join their fights.
  • Each story is its own world with its own character — switch between them any time, your keys stay yours.
  • No accounts, no email. Your character is a Nostr keypair — own it, bring it anywhere.
  • Works offline once loaded. Your moves queue and sync when you're back online.

What is Nostr? A decentralized, signed-event protocol — like email for cryptographic identities. Taleforge uses it as a transport: every action you take is a signed event broadcast over public relays, so other players see you, your character is portable, and stories can be published without any central server. You don't need to know anything about Nostr to play. Just save your nsec.
